MCIEF 2025

📅 April 14–17, 2025 📍 Orlando
← Back to Events

MyTrucksure is excited to sponsor and exhibit at the MCIEF 2025 Annual Conference, taking place from April 14 to 17.

This conference will showcase innovations and provide valuable insights into the transportation insurance industry. It will also offer networking opportunities for leaders, visionaries, and experts within the motor industry.

For event registration, please visit: https://mcief.org/events-2/2025-annual-conference-in-person-orlando/

We invite you to visit our exhibition table to learn more about our specialized app for the trucking insurance market, MyTrucksure™ SaaS.
